Supplemental
Fund Claims

Supplemental Fund Claims are intended for sources that:

  1. Were "unimpacted" (i.e., there was no measureable concentration of PFAS detected in the water source) at the time of filing the action fund claim but have since exhibited detections of PFAS; OR

  2. Exhibited PFOA and PFOS detections below the proposed Federal MCL of 4 ppt (ng/L) at the time of filing the action fund claim but have since had a PFOA or PFOS detection above 4 ppt.
