Supplemental
Fund Claims

Supplemental Fund Claims are intended for sources that:

  1. Were "unimpacted" (i.e., there was no measureable concentration of PFAS detected in the water source) at the time of filing the action fund claim but have since exhibited detections of PFAS; OR

  2. Exhibited PFAS detections below the Proposed Federal PFAS MCLs or an applicable State PFAS MCL at the time of filing the action fund claim, but have since had one or more PFAS detections above the Proposed Federal PFAS MCLs or applicable State PFAS MCLs.
