This notification is for any Public Water System (PWS) that is attempting to use this portal in which Environmental Health & Engineering (EH&E) has not been provided appropriate PWS information. To provide said information, please login to the EH&E Forms Website — using the same credentials you entered for this site — and complete the following:

  1. The "PWS Characteristics" form;
  2. A "Source Data" form for each of your Sources. This includes any active or inactive source that was used now, or in the past, for drinking water.
