PFAS Results

Below you will provide all relevant information about PFAS detections within this source. You will be asked if the source has had any detections of PFOA, PFOS, PFHxS, GenX, PFNA, or PFBS, since these are the six analytes that are part of the "Proposed Federal MCLs" referenced in the 3M/DuPont and Tyco/BASF settlements. If this source has exhibited a detection of a different PFAS analyte AND has a higher concentration compared to all of the six analytes listed above, please indicate this in the 'Highest Other PFAS Results' section below.
