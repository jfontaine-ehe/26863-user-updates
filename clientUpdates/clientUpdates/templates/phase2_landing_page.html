Source Forms

Please complete a "Source Data" form for each of your Water Sources. As defined by the settlements, a Water Source is:

  • A groundwater well, with sampling done at the wellhead.
  • A surface water treatment plant (supplied by one or more surface water sources or surface water combined with groundwater), with sampling done at the entry point into the treatment plant.
  • Used, now or in the past, for drinking water.

This includes any active or inactive Water Source that was used now, or in the past, for drinking water. This form is meant to collect information including (but not limited to) the Water Source's maximum flow rate, annual production data, and PFAS data.
